# 🚲 TravelHub — Bike to Work & TaxSaver Tickets

> **Portal:** [gong.travelhub.ie](https://gong.travelhub.ie) — register with your Gong work email.

TravelHub is Gong's self-service workplace travel benefits platform.

## 🚲 Bike to Work Scheme

- Save **up to 52%** on bikes and accessories via salary sacrifice
- Tax and National Insurance savings — no upfront payment required

## 🚆 TaxSaver Tickets

- Save on public transport commuting costs through payroll deductions

## ⚠️ Things to Note

- Monthly salary deductions apply during the selected repayment period
- If you leave before repayments are complete, the remaining balance may need to be settled

## How to Apply

1. Go to [gong.travelhub.ie](https://gong.travelhub.ie)
2. Register with your Gong work email
3. Log in → personal dashboard → complete application
