# 👤 Meeting Participant

External participant (customer / prospect) who clicks the consent page join link from their calendar invite. Sees the Gong-hosted consent page before being redirected to Zoom/Teams/Webex. Can **Accept** (recording allowed), **Decline** (recording suppressed), or receive a pre-call consent email and respond before the meeting.
