# 🧑 Meeting Participant

> [[Call Scheduling/Canvas/Consent/Consent - Data Flow.canvas|← Canvas]]

The human who lands on the **jump page** (consent page) before a recorded meeting — via a jump-page URL
(`profileKey/userKey[/meetingKey]`) or a pre-call consent email link — and **accepts** or **skips**.
